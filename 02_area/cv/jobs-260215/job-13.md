Background

The world around us is experiencing significant change, the greatest in generations.

At Motion Applied, we’re accelerating the response to that change. Our vision is that our people and technologies will pioneer a better future. Our mission is to achieve breakthroughs in performance.

We’re at the cutting edge of innovation in industries including transportation and motorsport. We create solutions that drive efficiency and cost-effectiveness. We want to help to reduce the carbon footprint and become more sustainable.

This role is in the Connected Intelligence Business unit. Our products have been developed from Formula One technology to provide connectivity services and analytics to a range of customers, primarily in Transportation, but also in Industrial and Motorsport environments.

By applying our expertise, we deliver real benefits and pioneer a better future.

Driving Engineering Excellence

This isn’t just about writing great code; it’s about advocating practices that push high quality engineering. You’ll be in a team reflecting on what went well and what they could have done better and turn those into learning actions. You will take pride in the solutions you and your team develop.

You’ll be passionate about learning and continuous improvement, both for yourself and for your team. When you learn something new you will want to share it with others. You’ll organise and encourage learning opportunities for you and others and will actively advocate the growth of the knowledge of those around you.

The role

You will be one of our key contributors who guide and foster innovation by developing leading-edge products that span from the IoT edge to cloud applications.

You will work collaboratively to solve interesting and challenging problems in a cross functional team with other engineering disciplines including Data Scientists, Software Engineers, Test Engineers, Platform Engineers, Systems Engineers and Hardware Engineers.

The software team is missioned to build operational insight, control and analytics applications that manage our core connectivity services. In this team you'll have a unique opportunity to have first-hand exposure to the strategy of the company in key cloud and edge device initiatives, especially in building scalable and secure-by-design systems and solutions.

You also have opportunities to go through the whole lifecycle of products and services, are encouraged to participate in each phase, each part of the projects and have the whole picture of what you are working on.

As a Software Engineer who is passionate about technology, you will use the most effective and applicable technologies to create robust and scalable software that runs in the cloud or on the edge.

You will work on a variety of challenges towards product or solution deliverables, often highly complex and based around mission critical systems that combines capabilities like data streaming, aggregation, asset management, monitoring, reporting and visualisation, through clean and consistent coding.

Experience and understanding of building products on cloud infrastructure such as AWS, Azure and GCP and micro services.
Experience in building data pipelines and data storage solutions

Familiarity with message brokers.

Understanding of containerisation (Docker) and container orchestration (Kubernetes)
Basic understanding of security principals
Good understanding of agile or lean development practices.
Ability to write and speak in English fluently.
